FAQ

Your questions

If something's not here, just ask.

Talk to us →
Do we need to be technical? +
No. We handle the accounts, integrations, and configuration. You learn how to use what we've built, which we make as simple as clicking a button and typing. Most clients have never set up a piece of software before and it's completely fine.
How long before we're up and running? +
Most teams are using AI tools within 2–4 weeks of the first call. More complex setups — connecting AI to an existing CRM or booking system — can take 4–6 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline after the audit, not a sales estimate.
Which tools do you work with? +
ChatGPT (OpenAI), Claude (Anthropic), Zapier, Make.com, Notion AI, Google Workspace AI, and others. We pick what fits your budget and workflows — not what's trending. Sometimes one tool is the right answer. Sometimes it's three.
Is our data safe? +
We only configure tools that meet Hong Kong's data privacy requirements. Your business data doesn't train any AI models. For healthcare and accounting clients, we have specific protocols. We walk you through exactly what goes where before anything is set up.
Our team resists new software. Is that a problem? +
It's common. We've helped teams where half the staff were sceptical when we walked in. Our workshops show rather than tell — we find the one task that makes your most resistant person's job easier and demonstrate it live. Most people come around once they see it working for them.
Can we do this in Cantonese? +
係！我哋提供廣東話服務。All calls, training sessions, and support run in Cantonese, Mandarin, or English — whichever your team prefers.
We're in a regulated industry. Can you handle that? +
Yes. We've worked with healthcare, accounting, and legal clients. We won't recommend any setup that creates compliance risk — that's not negotiable, regardless of how useful a tool might otherwise be.
What does the monthly retainer include? +
One strategy call per month, unlimited support with a 24-hour response time, tool updates as AI keeps changing, and suggestions when we spot new ways to help your business. You also get access to our client resource library. One part-time AI department without the full-time cost.
